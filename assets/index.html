Emoji Story

Explain yourself!

Do you like emojis? Have you ever wanted to write a story using emojis as your inspiration? Well now you can! Emoji Story is a fun game where you can create unique stories to add to an emoji. Amaze your friends, annoy your friends, inspire your friends. Either way Emoji Story will help you achieve your wildest dreams.

Instructions

  1. Click the checkered flag to get started.
  2. Press the start button. The timer will begin.
  3. Be inspired by the emoji to write a gut wrenching story. But you only have 25 seconds, so type fast!
  4. When complete, we will use computer magic to compile your story.
  5. Share with friends!
